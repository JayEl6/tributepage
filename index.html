Daniel Jacob Radcliffe

Timeline

  • 1989 - Born July 23 in London, United Kingdom
  • 1995 - A british actor who began acting at the age of six and first appeared as a monkey in a school play.
  • 1999 - Made his acting debut at the age of ten in the BBC one television film David Copperfield
  • 2001 - Rose to fame and won his first award at the age of twelve, when he began portraying Harry Potter in the film series of the same name Harry Potter.
  • 2002 - Continously act and won many awards as Harry Potter until the year 2009 in the film series of the same name.
  • 2010 - Recieves the Teen Choice Award and continually acting on other Harry Potter short stories until year 2011.
  • 2011 - Portrayed more movie characters like J Peirrepont Finch in the Broadway rivival of the musical How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying.
  • 2012 - Played the lawyer Arthur Kipps in the horror film The Woman in Black and won the Empire Award for Best Horror.
  • 2013 - Played the role of Ig Perrish in the Horns, starred in the romantic comedy What if? and in Martin McDognagh's dark comedy The Cripple of Inishmaan until year 2014.
  • 2015 - Radcliffe starred in the movies Victor Frankenstein, The Gamechangers and Trainwreck
  • 2016 - Radcliffe was part of the satirical plays Provacy and in the American heist thriller film Now You See Me 2.
  • 2017 - Played the role of Yossi Ghinsberg in the Jungle a adventure/drama movie and was part of some 2017 film like Lost in London and shows like National Theatre Live.
  • 2018 - Played the main role in the action/crime movie Beast of Burden.
  • 2019 - Radcliffe act as the voice actor of Rex Dasher in the Family/Adventure movie Playmobil and in the same year he played the main role in the movie Guns Akimbo.
  • 2020 - Radcliffe also was part of the films like Escape from Pretoria and a netflix film the Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt: Kimmy vs. The Reverenda.
  • 2021 - Radcliffe once again was part of the recent Harry Potter 20th Anniversary: Return to Hogwarts.
  • 2022 - At the age of 33 Radcliffe is continously being part of some movies, tv shows and recently was part of the movie The Lost City.
